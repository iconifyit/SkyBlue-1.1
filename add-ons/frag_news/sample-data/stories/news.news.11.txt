<p>SkyBlueCanvas can now support for Google Gadgets via the Gadgets extension written by Scott Lewis, the developer of SkyBlueCanvas.</p><p>Web sites can be extended endlessly through the use of Gadgets and Widgets. Google Gadgets work very well with SkyBlueCanvas because the Plugin API is extremely flexible and allows a developer to add functionality well beyond that which is built into the core of the system.</p><p>With this new extension, the possibilities for customization of a SkyBlueCanvas site are almost limitless. There are thousands of Google Gadgets available at <a title="Link to http://www.google.com/ig/directory?synd=open" href="http://www.google.com/ig/directory?synd=open">www.google.com/ig/directory</a></p>
